Transcribing

To transcribe a group of forms select their checkboxes and then choose one of the transcribe actions from the action menu. Form view will show interactive images of entire forms one at a time. Table view will show images of segments from all the chosen forms in a table.

Filtering

By selecting filters from the panel on the left you can apply actions to all the forms matching some criteria. For example, you could generate a csv with only the transcribed forms in batch X that use template Y.
